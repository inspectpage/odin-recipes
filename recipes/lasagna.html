Lasagna

This lasagna is a homemade recipe that's been passed on from generations to generation. It's incredibly delicious and hopefully you think so too!

  1. 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  2. ¾ pound lean ground beef
  3. ½ cup minced onion
  4. 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  5. 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
