Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna, the most scrumptious dinner you'll ever have!

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy ricotta or b√©chamel, and melted cheese, typically mozzarella. Baked to perfection, it offers a comforting, hearty meal with a perfect blend of savory flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
