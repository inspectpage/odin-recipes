Pho Recipe

Pho, the most scrumptious dinner you'll ever have!

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese soup made with a flavorful broth, rice noodles, tender slices of beef or chicken, and fresh herbs like cilantro and basil. Served with lime wedges and chili for added zest, it's a warm, aromatic dish perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Beef bones and tough beef cuts
  • Flat rice noodles
  • Aromatics
  • Spices
  • Daikon
  • Salt, sugar, and fish sauce
  • Steak

Steps

  1. Simmer beef bones with salt in a large pot for 2 hours.
  2. Roast onions and ginger under the broiler until charred, then chop the onions and peel/slice the ginger.
  3. Skim fat from the broth, then add oxtail, radish, onions, and a spice bouquet (ginger, anise, cinnamon, peppercorns, cloves). Simmer for 4+ hours.
  4. Strain the broth, chill overnight, and skim the fat.
  5. Reheat the broth, soak rice noodles in hot water until tender, and thinly slice frozen sirloin.
  6. Divide noodles and sirloin into bowls, then pour hot broth over to cook the beef. Serve!
