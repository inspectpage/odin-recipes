Steak Recipe

Steak, the most scrumptious dinner you'll ever have!

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy ricotta or béchamel, and melted cheese, typically mozzarella. Baked to perfection, it offers a comforting, hearty meal with a perfect blend of savory flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 pound) flat iron steak
  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh chives
  • ¼ cup Cabernet Sauvignon (or other dry red wine)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dry mustard powder

Steps

  1. Place steak in a resealable bag and add a marinade of olive oil, garlic, herbs, red wine, salt, pepper, and mustard powder.
  2. Seal the bag, removing excess air, and marinate in the fridge for 2-3 hours.
  3. Sear steak in a hot skillet for 3-4 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Discard the marinade, let the steak rest for 5 minutes, then serve.
  6. Slice the steak and enjoy with your favorite sides.
