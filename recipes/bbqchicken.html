BBQ chicken

Juicy BBQ chicken grilled to perfection and coated in a smoky, sweet, and tangy barbecue sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in or boneless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 375–400°F / 190–200°C). If baking, preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Brush with olive oil and season both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika.
  3. Place chicken on the grill and cook for 5–7 minutes per side, or until nicely charred and almost cooked through.
  4. Brush a layer of barbecue sauce over the chicken and cook for another 2–3 minutes per side, basting occasionally until caramelized and fully cooked (internal temperature 165°F / 74°C).
  5. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes to lock in juices.
  6. Brush with extra barbecue sauce before serving, and garnish with chopped parsley.
