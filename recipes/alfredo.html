Chicken Alfredo

A creamy and comforting Chicken Alfredo recipe made with tender chicken breast, rich Parmesan sauce, and fettuccine pasta — perfect for a hearty dinner.

Ingredients

  • oz (340 g) fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • ½ cup cooked broccoli or spinach (optional)
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta — Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook fettuccine according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the chicken — Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Cook the chicken — In a large skillet over medium heat, add olive oil. Cook chicken for 5–7 minutes per side or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove from skillet and slice into strips.
  4. Make the Alfredo sauce — In the same skillet, melt butter. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add cream and cheese — Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg if desired.
  6. Combine — Add the cooked pasta and chicken back into the skillet. Toss until everything is coated in sauce. Add broccoli or spinach if using.
  7. Serve — Garnish with chopped parsley and extra Parmesan. Serve warm.
